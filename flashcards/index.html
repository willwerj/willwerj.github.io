CSV Format Guide

Overview

Each row in your CSV becomes one flashcard. The first row must be a header with exact column names.

Required Columns

ColumnDescription
questionThe front of the card
answerThe back of the card (revealed on flip)
card_typedefinition, true_false, or conceptual
concept_tagNamed concept (e.g. "Entropy")
chapterChapter or section label
source_fileSource filename or URL
page_numberPage number or N/A

LaTeX Support

Wrap inline math in $…$ and display math in $$…$$.

Formatting Rules

  • Fields containing commas must be wrapped in double quotes: "mean, variance"
  • To include a double quote inside a field, double it: "He said ""hi"""
  • question and answer must not be empty

Example

chapter,concept_tag,card_type,question,answer,source_file,page_number Chapter 1,Entropy,definition,What is entropy?,Average uncertainty of a distribution.,notes.pdf,5 Chapter 1,Binomial,true_false,"True or False: $\binom{n}{k}$ counts k-head sequences.","True.",notes.pdf,8
